BOSTON, MASSACHUSETTS

My boyfriend and I visited Boston from June 16 - June 18th, 2023. Boston pleasently surprised us and is definitely my favorite city in the US I have visited so far! Being from NYC, I didn't believe that anywhere in the US could top our food, but Boston proved me wrong. I had the freshest sushi and sashimi of my life here, as well as delicious lobster rolls and above average Italian food. The train ride to Boston from NYC is very scenic, especially seeing the shores in Conneticut.

Besides food, we did quite alot in Boston! We explored almost the entire city by foot in 2 days! We also went to Harvard! I definitely recommend as many museums as you can see here. I was so surprised by Harvard's natural history museum, it beats NYC's Museum of Natural History! My favorite part of that museum was the food history and rocks and minerals displays.
