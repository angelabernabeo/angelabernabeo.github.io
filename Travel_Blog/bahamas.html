NASSAU AND PARADISE ISLAND, THE BAHAMAS

My boyfriend and I visited The Bahamas from January 16 - January 21, 2023. We stayed at the Riu on Paradise Island and also did two day trips to Pearl Island and scuba diving. The ocean water was crystal clear and the bluest blue! Pearl Island was well worth the money and exploring Nassau is easy after walking over the bridge that connects Paradise Island!
