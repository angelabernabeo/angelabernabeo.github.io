BRUSSELS, BELGIUM

Brussels is my favorite city I have visited so far! We went on April 13, 2023 on the Eurostar from London. Brussels is amazing. We started the day off at the Atominium which had amazing exhibits in each sphere. Then, we went to Little Europe which was so cute! On our way to Grand Place, we stopped in countless churches, many which have amazing past and present art. The architecture was amazing and the street food was also delicious.
