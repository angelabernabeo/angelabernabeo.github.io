NIAGARA FALLS, CANADA

My family and I spent Saturday, July 28th in Niagara Falls on a tour from Toronto. The tour included roundtrip transportation from Toronto to Niagara, a lunch overlooking the Falls, the boat ride close up to the Falls, a winery with wine tasting, and tour of Niagara on the Lake. The photos of the Falls do not do it justice. They are so much bigger in person than I expected!
